Brownies

Desciption:

Brownies. Some of the most loving foods one can eat. They are filled with sugar and love. They can be the best food to bring to a get together due to their chocoloate and flavorful nature. Follow me as I lead you on to how to create these amazing treats!

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cocoa powder
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Baking powder

Steps:

    Step 1:

  1. Melt the butter on the stove

    2. Step 2:

  2. Remove from heat and add the remaining wet ingredients.

    3. Step 3:

  3. Beat in the dry ingredients

    4. Step 4:

  4. Spread the batter in the pan and bake.
