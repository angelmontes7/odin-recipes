Tacos

Description:

Making Tacos Dorados is very easy and simple. Tacos are a delicous food that can be made by anyone. Many people make there tacos in different ways. That is the beatiful thing about this food. It can be made in all sorts of ways Come along with me as I teach you how to make tacos and what ingredients you will need to accomplish this.

Ingredients:

  • Tortillas
  • Tomatos
  • Cabbage
  • Onions
  • Beef
  • Seasonings

Steps:

  1. Get your lean ground sirloin and put it in the pan.
    • There will be grease that comes off from the beef.
    • Use that in order to fry your tortillas
  2. The grease that the meat will give off will be the grease used in order to fry your tortillas.
  3. Make sure to season the ground beef.
  4. Once both are finished cooking you will need to chop up cabage, onions, and tomatos.
  5. Then for the easy part you put it all together with the porportions that you desire!
